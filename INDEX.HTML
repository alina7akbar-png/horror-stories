THE THING UNDER THE BED

It was nearly midnight when Sana returned to her hostel room after studying in the library. It was nearly midnight when Sana returned to her hostel room after studying in the library. The corridor was unusually silent. Even the tube light outside her room flickered on and off. As she opened the door, she noticed something strange. Her roommate’s chair was slowly moving on its own. She froze. “Maybe the window is open,” she whispered to herself. But the window was locked. Trying to ignore it, she quickly changed clothes and lay down on her bed. A few minutes later, she heard a soft knocking sound. Tok… Tok… Tok… Not on the door. From under her bed. Her heartbeat became faster. She gathered courage, switched on her phone flashlight, and slowly bent down to look underneath. Nothing. Relieved, she stood up — and suddenly noticed muddy footprints on the floor. They started from under her bed… …and ended right beside her pillow. Her hands began shaking. She immediately called her roommate. “Where are you?” Sana asked nervously. There was silence for a moment. Then her roommate replied in a trembling voice, “I’m outside the hostel… Sana, listen carefully… I forgot to tell you something.” “What?” “The girl who stayed in our room before us…” “She died last year.” At that exact moment, Sana felt someone breathing behind her.
